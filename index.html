Announcement:

We are updating how the application models stand dynamics and product output. The current stand-level green weight model and merchantable product breakout function are being replaced with a stand table projection system using a Weibull distribution, giving users more control over merchandising decisions.

Road Map:

  • Add Western Gulf model (coming soon)
  • Implement Weibull distribution and stand table system (coming soon)
  • Overhaul product specifications with a built-in merchandiser (coming soon)
  • Validate and document silvicultural treatment responses (In progress)
  • Incorporate uncertainty with propagation of error into future projections (Planned)
  • Incorporate optimization for management regime selection (Planned)
